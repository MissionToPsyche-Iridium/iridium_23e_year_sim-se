How Psyche's name came to be

Introduction

Psyche is a metal-rich asteroid named after the Greek goddess of the soul.

Historical Background

Discovered in 1852 by Italian astronomer Annibale de Gasparis, Psyche is one of the largest asteroids.

NASA's Psyche Mission

NASA's Psyche spacecraft will study the asteroid to learn about planetary cores.

Fun Facts

  • Psyche is primarily composed of nickel and iron.
  • It is about 226 kilometers (140 miles) in diameter.
  • The name "Psyche" means "soul" in Greek.
