    Reason for the mission

    Scientists think Psyche may consist of significant amounts of metal from the core of a planetesimal, one of the building blocks of our solar system.

    The asteroid is most likely a survivor of multiple violent hit-and-run collisions, common when the solar system was forming

    Psyche may be able to tell us how Earth's core and the cores of the other rocky, or terrestrial, planets came to be.
