A Year on 16 Psyche

What would life be like on Psyche?

Select a Year

Day Length

The day is only 4.5 horus so you will need to get everything done that you need to do in about 3 hours to save one hour to sleep.

If some professions work 12 hour shifts, that would be working three straight days on Psyche without going to sleep.

One meal a day

You might only have time to eat one meal a day on Psyche.

If you eat lunch at 12pm noon and then dinner at 5pm, you might have skipped an entire day on Psyche without eating!

You might need to eat meals much quicker to save as much time in the day as you can.

School Days

School days on Psyche would be around 1 hour if they were relative to those school days on earth.

But, the school year would be 5 earth years long! If you started kindergarden on Psyche as the same time as students on Earth, when you finished 1st grade, the earth students would have finished 5th grade.
